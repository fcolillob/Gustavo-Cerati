Biography

Gustavo Adrián Cerati (11 August 1959 – 4 September 2014) was an Argentine musician, singer-songwriter and record producer, who gained international recognition for being the leader, vocalist, composer and guitarist of the rock band Soda Stereo. He is widely considered by critics, specialized press and musicians as one of the most important and influential artists of Latin rock. Rolling Stone magazine ranked Cerati as the 33rd best rock singer of all time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who was Gustavo Cerati?
Gustavo Cerati was an Argentine musician known as the frontman of Soda Stereo, an influential Spanish rock band.
What were Gustavo Cerati's major achievements?
Cerati achieved success with Soda Stereo, one of the most significant Latin American rock bands. He also had a successful solo career.
What was Gustavo Cerati's legacy in music?
His legacy includes his influence on Latin American music, his ability to blend styles, and his impact on contemporary culture, both musically and artistically.
